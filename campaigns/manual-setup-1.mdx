---
title: "Manual Campaigns"
description: "How to send bulk messages using the Gliiv Dashboard."
---

Manual campaigns allow you to reach your audience without writing a single line of code.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your Data">
    Ensure your contact list is in **CSV** or **Excel** format. At a minimum, you need a column for phone numbers (e.g., in `+250...` format).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload to Dashboard">
    Navigate to the **Campaigns** tab and click **"New Campaign"**. Drag and drop your file into the upload zone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Columns">
    Our system will automatically detect your columns. Map the "Phone Number" field and any custom variables (like Names) you want to use in your message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Launch">
    Check the preview of your message. Once satisfied, hit **"Launch"**. Our backend queue (BullMQ) will handle the heavy lifting instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Pro Tip:** Use matte-finish branding colors in your dashboard reports for a professional look when sharing results with your team.
</Info>
