## The Campaign Lifecycle

Understanding how Gliiv processes your data helps you manage your communications more effectively:

1. **Staging & Validation:** Your data is ingested and checked for correct formatting (e.g., phone number structure).
2. **Processing:** Messages are queued and prepared for dispatch using our high-scale processing engine.
3. **Dispatch:** Messages are sent through our carrier gateways.
4. **Tracking:** Monitor delivery status and engagement in real-time through the dashboard.
