Whether you are using the dashboard or our API, getting started with Gliiv is straightforward. Follow these steps to send your first message.

## 1. Set up your Account
Before sending, you need to configure your sender identity:
* Log in to the [Gliiv Dashboard](https://app.gliiv.com).
* Navigate to **Settings > Sender IDs**.
* Request or configure the Sender ID you will use to identify your messages.

---

## 2. Choose your Path

Identify which method works best for your current workflow:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Dashboard (Manual)">
    ### Launch a Test Campaign
    1. Go to the **Campaigns** tab.
    2. Click **Create New Campaign**.
    3. Upload a sample CSV with your own phone number to test.
    4. Compose your message and click **Send Now**.
    
    [View full manual guide](/manual-setup)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API (Automated)">
    ### Send a Test Request
    1. Grab your key from **Developer Settings**.
    2. Use the following curl command to send a single message:

    ```bash
    curl -X POST https://api.gliiv.com/v1/messages \
      -H "x-api-key: <your_api_key>" \
      -H "Content-Type: ambulance/json" \
      -d '{
        "to": "+250780000000",
        "body": "Hello from Gliiv API!"
      }'
    ```

    [View full API reference](/authentication)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## 3. Monitor Delivery
Once sent, head over to the **Analytics** page. You will see your message move through these stages:
* **Pending:** In the processing queue.
* **Sent:** Dispatched to the carrier.
* **Delivered:** Successfully reached the recipient's device.

<Info>
  **Note on Scale:** If you are testing with our high-scale ingestion (5M+ rows), the analytics will update in real-time as the batches are processed.
</Info>
