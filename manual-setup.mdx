Manual campaigns are the fastest way to send bulk messages without writing code. Our system is optimized to handle massive contact lists with high precision.

## Preparing your Contact List

Before uploading, ensure your file (CSV or Excel) is formatted correctly.

* **Phone Numbers:** Must include the country code (e.g., `250...`).
* **Placeholders:** You can include extra columns like `First_Name` or `Balance` to personalize your messages using `{{Variable}}` syntax.

<Info>
  **Pro Tip:** If your file is extremely large (e.g., 5 million rows), our ingestion engine will process it in the background using BullMQ, allowing you to continue using the dashboard while we prepare the queue.
</Info>

---

## The Creation Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your File">
    Navigate to **Campaigns > New Campaign**. Drag your CSV or Excel file into the upload zone. Gliiv will instantly begin validating the data format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mapping Attributes">
    Match your file columns to Gliiv attributes. 
    * **Recipient:** Map your phone number column.
    * **Variables:** Map any custom columns you wish to use as placeholders in your message body.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose & Schedule">
    Type your message in the editor. Below the message box, you have two options for timing:
    * **Send Now:** Messages enter the queue immediately.
    * **Schedule for Later:** Toggle the radio button to select a specific date and time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Launch">
    Check the preview. Once ready, click **Launch Campaign**. 
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 📅 Campaign Scheduling

If you aren't ready to send immediately, you can use the **Scheduling** feature.

### How it Works
When you check the **Schedule for Later** radio button, a date and time picker will appear.

* **Lead Time:** Scheduled campaigns must be set for at least **3 hours from the current time**. This ensures our system has ample time to validate and stage large-scale data before the dispatch window opens.
* **Timezone:** All campaigns are scheduled based on your account's local timezone settings.

### Managing Scheduled Campaigns
You can view, edit, or cancel a scheduled campaign from the **Campaigns** list view at any time before the scheduled dispatch time. Look for the **"Scheduled"** status badge.
